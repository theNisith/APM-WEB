About APM ASSOCIATES

Our Story

Established in 2014, APM ASSOCIATES began as a proprietorship business focused on supplying machine components. Over the years, we have evolved and expanded, culminating in our incorporation as APM ASSOCIATES (Private) Limited in 2023. This transformation marked a significant milestone, enabling us to broaden our scope to include engineering consultancy services and the installation of heavy machinery and equipment across various industries.

Services We Offer

  • Supplying spares for heavy machinery, manufacturing plants, equipment, specialized heavy-duty automobiles, and gantry cranes
  • Manufacturing plant installations
  • Generator and machinery setup
  • Consultancy services, including design and supervision of mechanical and electrical projects
  • Statutory compliance engineering

Our team of professionally qualified engineers is dedicated to assisting customers in resolving technical issues with their equipment and machinery. We provide tailored technical proposals to enhance overall efficiency and ensure optimal performance.

Message from the Managing Director

As a Sri Lankan citizen and a graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Engineering specializing in Mechanical Engineering, I have had the privilege of serving in various esteemed roles throughout my professional journey. I am a Chartered Engineer accredited by the Institution of Engineers of Sri Lanka, a Member of the Engineering Council of Sri Lanka, and a Chartered Automobile Engineer accredited by the Institute of Automotive Engineers of Sri Lanka.

My career began at the Export Development Board of Sri Lanka as a Packaging Engineer, followed by a tenure at the Sri Lanka Ports Authority, where I held positions such as Refit Engineer, Superintending Engineer (Marine), Chief Engineer (Mechanical Plants), and Resident Manager for the Port of Trincomalee. During my tenure as Resident Manager, I successfully transformed the Port of Trincomalee from a loss-making entity into a self-financing port.

Today, I am committed to leading APM ASSOCIATES (Private) Limited to deliver expert engineering services and procurement solutions to our clients in both the public and private sectors. Our mission is to provide innovative, reliable, and efficient solutions that meet the highest industry standards.
